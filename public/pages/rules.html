2.5. Mercs

A player not rostered, but used instead of an unavailable team member is considered a mercenary. Eligible mercenaries require a Qixalite league account and no active punishments.

A maximum of two mercenaries are allowed, but merc usage is limited to four matches per season and a team is restricted to use an individual player twice as a merc per season.

Message the SteamID and Qixalite league account to a member of the staff team; this will begin the process to authorise the player for the match. Submitting mercenary requests with players that are in a higher skill bracket, will result in the request being denied.

Approved requests will require you to post the SteamID and Qixalite league account in the appropriate match thread. Failing to follow this process could lead to a mercenary ban for your team.

2.6. Forfeits

2.6.1. Win / Loss

Rule breaches during an official game and no contact or attempts to organise the match will result in a forfeit win to the victim team. Furthermore, both teams will have a maximum of 15 minutes past the agreed upon time to be readied.

Server organisation is at the fault of both teams, but is expected that the home team setup and send the server details to the away team. However, if this has not happened, the away team must book a server and try to provide server details. Similarly, the home team will have 15 minutes past to join the server or provide server details.

Forfeit wins require chat logs or evidence suggesting an intent to organise the match, but with no communication back from the other team. Post all evidence into the match thread when submitting the result.

A forfeit loss will count towards a forfeit punishment.

Point Amount: 4

2.6.2. Mutual

Instances where both teams have not organised or come to an agreement on the match times by Monday 11:59 PM - AEST will result in a mutual forfeit. Addition to this, if no scores have been submitted by either team, the match will also result in a mutual forfeit.

The exception to the rule is if both teams have contacted staff in regards to match organisational difficulties, then match negotiations will be extended at the discretion of the the staff team. However, if mediation is required a staff member will decide to force a time where both teams will have the best chances to field as many players as possible.

A mutual forfeit will count towards a forfeit punishment.

Point Amount: 0

2.6.3. Technical

Issues resulting in the inability for both teams being able to play the match will result in a technical forfeit. Such instances that could occur would be widespread power outages, steam outages and server booking issues.

A technical forfeit will not count towards a forfeit punishment.

Point Amount: 2

3. Punishments

3.1. Forfeits

Mutual and loss forfeits add up towards a forfeit punishment. Both types of forfeits equally count as 1 forfeit loss. Punishments for this type of offense will be outlined in the criteria below.

3.1.1. Early Forfeits

Early forfeits count as the first two weeks of regular season matches. If a team forfeits these two matches, they will be disqualified and removed from the league.

3.1.2. Total Forfeits

Total forfeits count as the entire amount of forfeits a team has taken throughout the season. Upon the third forfeit a team will be disqualified and removed from the league.

3.1.3. Play-off Forfeits

Play-off forfeits count as any match that occurs past the regular season. Any team that forfeits during a play-off match will also forfeit their right to receive prizes and badges for that season.

Forfeits during the play-offs will result in the team being disqualified and removed from the league. If possible the next highest seeded team will replace the team or another team in the play-offs when forfeit instances occur after quarter-finals.

Exceptions to the rule can be given depending on the reason of why the team was unable to field the required amount of players. If a team believes they will be unable to field players during the play-off period, contact the staff team to have your play-off position spot given to another team. Doing this will avoid the punishment and you will still receive participant badges.

3.7. Account Usage

3.7.1. Alternate accounts

Alternative accounts are prohibited to be used. All players are required to play with a single Steam account and to be registered with that account to the Qixalite league.

Appointing a new Steam account to be used within the league will require authorisation from the staff team. Contact staff to begin the authorisation process.

3.7.2. Sharing

Usage of your account by another party in an official match is restricted. If found sharing the account, all people involved will be punished equally.

3.7.3. Impersonation

Aliasing as other players with the intent to mislead or have malicious intent upon others will result in significant disqualifications from the league.
